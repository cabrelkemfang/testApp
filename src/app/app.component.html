About Us

SWAG-PD is a 22 nd century Non-Governmental Organization which specializes in Public Speaking, Life Coaching, Personal Development, Leadership and Entrepreneurship, all of which are influential because the group is founded on the Precepts of God/Allah for Success. SWAG-PD is passionate about the emergence of Cameroon and Africa and believes it can be made possible by creating a new breed of leaders, Influential Leaders (ILs), who will be characterized by a strong Spirituality (the ability to go above the flesh) which will form the bases for them to become Good Person’s with Stamina (GPSs) who will move Africa from Mediocrity to Excellence. SWAG-PD assists Youths (between 15-35 years) in getting to their Full Potential by helping them find their Purpose and living it to the Fullest. To become part of SWAG-PD, the prospective Influential Leader is obliged to go through TIL’s Full Potential Programme (TFPP). SWAG-PD’s big vision is to put Jesus Christ at the centre of Cameroonian and African Leadership and Entrepreneurship to move them from Mediocrity to Excellence. SWAG-PD believes that Cameroon and Africa are the promised Nation and Continent respectively but that will only be possible with Jesus Christ at the centre of everything.
“SWAG-PD, CREATING INFLUENTIAL LEADERS”

Our Services

Influential Public Speaking (IPS)

Influential public speaking aims at helping the trainee become capable of communicating a visison. This is as a result of the fact that as co-creators and like the overall creator was talking at the beginning to create the world, we too should talk like that to create change. It is influential of course because it is founded on the Principles/Precepts of God/Allah.

Influential Life Coaching (ILC)

Life coaching is that aspect of SWAG-PD that enables any trainee/menber to get the required guidance towards his/her Full Potential. Just as God was a coach in the creating story, SWAG-PD coaches people to drive them towards perfection. Life Coaching in SWAG- PD is of course founded on the Principles of God/Allah, hence Influential.

Influetial Leadership

Leadership is influence and SWAG-PD believes that true influence comes from the inner person (the Holy Spirit) which is as a result of a Belief System (SWAG-PD’s Belief System is the Holy Bible) and this kind of leadership is that which provides followers with their Real Needs. This kind of leadership occurs only when the person is at least on the path to his/her Purpose or has already found Purpose. We teach trainees and assist our members to influence others with their Gift.

Influential Personal Development (IPD)

This component is the area of training and coaching in which SWAG-PD through its various operations helps trainees and members to organize and discover who they are. SWAG-PD believes that you cannot lead others without leading yourself hence the need to do a Personal development that permits you to find you Gift/Talent/Potential as well as your Purpose that puts you on the platform for Greatness. It of course is Influential.

Influential Entreprenurship

Man was created to be an Entrepreneur as expressed in Gen 1:28-30 when man was asked to manage everything on earth. Once there are different kinds of animals/people, there is bound to be conflict and such situations needs Problem Solvers/Entrepreneurs/Solution Finders. SWAG-PD trains and coaches people on how to maximize their Gifts/Talents/Potentials in a manner that will help solve problems. Worthy of not is the fact that SWAG-PD believes that Entrepreneurship is an evolution and not a revelation and that true Entrepreneurship can never be void of God/Allah/Buddha.

Our Programs

Conferences

SWAG-PD organizes conferences to meet the needs as made relevant by sorrounding conditions. These conferences could either be restricted or open but the overall aim is always to discuss ways in which we can move Cameroon & Africa forward amidst the surrounding challenges which make the journey interesting.

Training Workshops

SWAG-PD organizes a wide range of training workshops based on the need identified either by SWAG-PD or based on the recommendation or request of a partner, and individual, organization, company or group. Such workshops will have as objective to help these different individuals or units to maximize the Potentials/Talents/Gifts they have in order to get the best output.

TIL’s Full Potential Programme (TFPP)

TFPP is a 4months training programme hosted by SWAG - PD which aims at creating Influential Leaders. This is the entrance gate into SWAG – PD as any person who intends to join is mandated to go through the programme. It is a programme which intends to take the trainees from a “loser” to a winner, from a person of low self-esteem to one of high self- esteem and from an “existing” person to one who is “living” and ready to drive to his/her full potential. SWAG-PD trains one TFPP batch per year.

Seminars

SWAG-PD equally organizes seminars either as a result of a gap they have noticed or based on the request from an individual, organization, company or group. The main aim in such cases is to see how to help the trainess or participants pick up both theoritical and practical skills on how to drive to their Full Potential.

ICT Lessons

Upon strong consideration of a rapidly changing landscape especially in terms of Technology, SWAG-PD organizes programmes that permit youths to pick up basic skills in technology that will help them assert themselves and become more influential and successful. In special cases, such a program might be organized to address a special need as necessitated by the conditions that abound.
